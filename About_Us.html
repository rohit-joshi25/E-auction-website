About Us

The Christies

Welcome to CHRISTIES, the global destination for discerning collectors and enthusiasts of exquisite antiques. With a rich history and a reputation that spans the globe, we have established ourselves as the premier platform for buying and selling timeless treasures.

Our Story:

Founded in 2024 by passionate collector Moksh Sharma, Christie's was born out of a shared love for the beauty, craftsmanship, and history encapsulated in every antique. What started as a modest venture has now evolved into a powerhouse in the world of antiques, attracting connoisseurs, experts, and enthusiasts alike.

Global Reach:

At Christie's, we take pride in our worldwide influence. Our network spans across continents, connecting sellers with buyers from every corner of the globe. Whether you're in search of a rare European artifact, an Asian masterpiece, or an American classic, our platform is the meeting point for diverse cultures and timeless pieces.

Seamless Buying and Selling:

At Christie's, our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to provide a personalized and seamless experience, offering expert guidance, secure transactions, and reliable shipping worldwide. Our commitment to customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of collectors and sellers alike.
